# NarrativePulse Model Card

## Summary
NarrativePulse forecasts narrative shock probability, severity, and event
intensity from open event, attention, and macro data.

## Intended use
Use this model for research, monitoring, and early warning prototyping.
Do not treat it as a sole decision-maker.

## Training data
- GDELT Events
- Wikimedia Pageviews
- FRED macro time series

## Outputs
- shock probability
- severity forecast
- next-day event intensity
- calibrated confidence and conformal intervals

## Risks
Public data can be biased, delayed, or incomplete.
Attention spikes are not equivalent to real-world harm.
